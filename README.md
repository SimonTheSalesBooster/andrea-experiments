## What Andrea Does

Andrea is the Experimentation and Curiosity Advisor on the Strategy Sprints Board of Advisors. Inspired by Andrea Hill.. 35+ years scaling mid-sized companies, critical thinker, founder of Hill Management Group (StrategyWerx). She built her career on one question: "What else can we try?"

Her superpower: she never accepts the first answer. Every strategy, every tactic, every assumption is a hypothesis until tested. She is the scientific method applied to business decisions.

## Methodology

Scientific method applied to business:

1. **Extract Assumptions** .. Every strategy has hidden assumptions. Name them explicitly.
2. **Design the Experiment** .. Hypothesis, A/B setup, control, duration, success metric, setup effort.
3. **Prioritize by Learning Value** .. Reversibility risk, speed to learn, impact if wrong.
4. **Pick THE TEST** .. The single most valuable test to run this week.
5. **Delegate Execution** .. Hand off to the A/B Tests agent for copy tests via a self-improving loop.
6. **Track Results** .. Flag overdue experiments. "Did we run it? What did we learn?"

## Key Features

- Reads the full board cascade, identifies every proposal and assumption that can be tested
- Designs lean experiments with hypothesis, A/B setup, control group, duration, and success metric
- Delegates execution to the A/B Tests agent (copy tests run through the self-improving loop: generate, judge, deploy, measure, learn)
- Tracks running experiments and flags overdue ones.. nothing gets forgotten
- Prioritizes by learning value.. the experiments that would most change what you do next

## Example Output

```
ANDREA'S TEST: Test whether video walkthroughs get more replies than text diagnostics

HYPOTHESIS: Prospects reply at higher rates to personalized 90-sec Loom videos
than to written positioning diagnostics.

TEST:
  A (control): Current text diagnostic email to 10 new prospects
  B (variant): 90-sec Loom video walkthrough to 10 new prospects

SAMPLE: 20 prospects from this week's outreach list, randomly split
DURATION: 2 weeks
SUCCESS METRIC: Reply rate for B is >2x reply rate for A
SETUP EFFORT: 2 hours (record one template video, adjust outreach sequence)

WHY THIS ONE: Outreach is our highest-volume activity. If video doubles reply
rates, that compounds across every prospect we contact. The cost of NOT knowing
is months of suboptimal outreach.
```
